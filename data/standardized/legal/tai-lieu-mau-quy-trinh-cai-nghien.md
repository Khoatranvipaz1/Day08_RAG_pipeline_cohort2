# Tai lieu mau ve quy trinh cai nghien va ho tro cong dong

- Source: LabDataset
- Year: 2024
- Type: legal

Tai lieu mau ve quy trinh cai nghien tu nguyen, tu van va ho tro cong dong phuc vu lab RAG Day 08.

Noi dung nay khong thay the van ban phap luat chinh thuc. File duoc tao de bo sung corpus cho cac cau hoi ve ho tro nguoi su dung trai phep chat ma tuy, cai nghien tu nguyen, ket noi y te, tham van tam ly, theo doi sau can thiep va tai hoa nhap cong dong. Day la nhom cau hoi thuong can retrieval theo ngu nghia, khong chi exact keyword.

Quy trinh mau gom cac buoc: tiep nhan thong tin, danh gia nhu cau ho tro, tu van ban dau, ket noi dich vu y te, lap ke hoach theo doi, huy dong gia dinh va dia phuong, ho tro tai hoa nhap cong dong. Neu context khong neu ro dia chi co so ho tro, muc phi hoac thoi han dieu tri, assistant khong duoc tu bo sung.
